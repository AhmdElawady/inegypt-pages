Frequently Asked Questions

Does InEgypt work offline?

Yes. InEgypt Pro users have full offline access to transit routes, schedules, attraction details, and city guides. All data is stored on your device — no internet required.

What transit systems are supported?

InEgypt covers Metro, BRT, LRT, Monorail, Tram, Train, and Bus networks across Egypt. Intercity travel by bus, train, and flight is also supported.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings in the app, tap "Your Plan", then tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

The app shows incorrect route information. What should I do?

Transit data is updated regularly, but schedules can change. Please email us with the route details and we'll investigate and update the data.

Which languages are supported?

InEgypt is available in English, Arabic, French, German, Spanish, Italian, Russian, Turkish, and Chinese.

How do I change the app language?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Language" to choose from the available languages.
