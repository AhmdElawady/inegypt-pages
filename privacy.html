Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

InEgypt is developed by Ahmed Elawady, an independent developer based in Egypt. This policy explains how the InEgypt iOS application handles your information.

The short version: InEgypt is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect personal data, we do not track you, and we do not use any analytics services.

1. Information We Collect

InEgypt collects minimal information, only what is necessary to provide its core features:

  • Location Data — When you grant permission, the app accesses your location while the app is in use to find nearby transit stops and calculate routes from your current position. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server.
  • Device Information (Support Only) — If you choose to contact support via the in-app email feature, your device model, iOS version, and app version are included in the email to help us assist you. This only happens when you explicitly send a support email.
  • Language Preference — Your selected app language is stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not require account creation or registration
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or personal identifiers
  • We do not use analytics or tracking services of any kind
  • We do not track your behavior across apps or websites
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties
  • We do not collect background location data

3. How We Use Information

The limited information described above is used exclusively for:

  • Calculating transit routes from your current location
  • Displaying nearby transit stops and attractions
  • Providing proximity alerts for nearby points of interest (when enabled)
  • Managing local cache on your device

4. Data Storage

The app stores certain data on your device to provide its features:

  • Favorites & Preferences — Stored in your device's local storage
  • Cached Images — Temporarily stored on your device; can be cleared from Settings
  • App Content — Transit and attraction data is included within the app to support its core functionality. Access to certain features and data may require an active internet connection depending on your plan.

No personal data is stored on external servers under our control.

5. Third-Party Services

InEgypt uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple MapKit — For map display and place search. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — For processing in-app purchases. All payment information is handled entirely by Apple. We never see or store your payment details.
  • Apple CloudKit — For loading attraction images and content. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple WeatherKit — For weather information. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Nuke (Open Source) — An image loading library that caches images locally on your device. It does not collect or transmit any data.

6. In-App Purchases

InEgypt offers optional one-time purchases (Plus and Lifetime Access). All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Purchase records are managed by Apple and can be viewed in your App Store account.

7. Children's Privacy

InEgypt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal data from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

8. Location Permission

InEgypt requests location access only with the "While Using the App" permission level. The app never requests "Always" location access and does not track your location in the background. You can revoke location permission at any time in your device's Settings, and the app will continue to function — you will simply need to enter locations manually.

9. Data Security

Transit data files are encrypted and stored within the app bundle. All network communication with Apple services uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Since we do not operate servers or collect user data, there is no centralized database that could be compromised.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
